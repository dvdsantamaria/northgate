FAQs

Is the guide useful if I already have plans or an architect?
Yes. The checker validates timeline risk from council, services, access, procurement and builder readiness. It often highlights delays not covered in drawings.
Do I need to be building right now to download it?
No. It is designed for planning. You can use it while comparing sites or before lodging a DA to understand likely windows.
How accurate are the timelines?
Estimates come from live pipeline data and over 100 finished projects in Sydney. We include buffers for approvals and lead times so results are realistic.
How much does it cost to build a custom home?
Cost varies by site, spec and approvals. After you run the checker you can request a budget range and inclusions sheet. No obligation.
Can you help with the design process too?
Yes. We coordinate design with trusted architects and engineers or we work with your team. The goal is clear decisions, fewer variations and a clean start.
